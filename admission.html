Admission Information

Welcome to Paradise Academy

At Paradise Academy, we are committed to providing an outstanding educational experience for our students. Our admission process is designed to ensure that we enroll students who are well-suited to thrive in our community.

Admission Requirements

To apply for admission, please ensure that you meet the following requirements:

  • Completed application form
  • Official transcripts from previous schools
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Personal statement or essay
  • Interview with the admissions committee

Application Process

Our application process is straightforward and can be completed in a few steps:

  • Submit the online application form
  • Upload required documents
  • Schedule an interview
  • Receive admission decision

Important Dates

Please keep the following important dates in mind when applying:

  • Application Deadline: January 31
  • Interview Dates: February 15 - March 15
  • Admission Decision: April 1
